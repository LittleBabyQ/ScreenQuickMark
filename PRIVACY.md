# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Screen QuickMark is a lightweight Windows screen annotation tool developed by BabyQ.

This privacy policy explains how Screen QuickMark handles user data.

## Summary

Screen QuickMark is designed to work locally on your device.

We do not collect, upload, sell, share, or analyze your personal data.

## Data Collection

Screen QuickMark does not collect any personal information.

Screen QuickMark does not collect:

- your name;
- your email address;
- your account information;
- your device identifier;
- your location;
- your contacts;
- your browsing history;
- your screen content;
- your screenshots;
- your annotation content;
- your usage analytics.

## Screen Content and Annotations

Screen QuickMark allows you to draw and add text annotations on top of your screen.

All annotation operations are processed locally on your device.

Screen QuickMark does not upload your screen content, screenshots, drawings, text annotations, or any other visual content to any server.

## Network Access

Screen QuickMark does not require an account and does not rely on a cloud service for its core functionality.

The application does not intentionally transmit user data to the developer or to third-party services.

## Analytics and Tracking

Screen QuickMark does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking cookies, telemetry services, or third-party tracking tools.

## Local Files

Screen QuickMark may create or use local files only when required for normal application operation, such as local configuration, application assets, or user-initiated exports if such features are available.

Any such files remain on your device unless you choose to share them yourself.

## Third-Party Services

Screen QuickMark does not integrate third-party services for analytics, advertising, or cloud synchronization.

If future versions add optional third-party integrations, this privacy policy will be updated before such features are released.

## Children's Privacy

Screen QuickMark does not knowingly collect data from children or from any other users.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated when Screen QuickMark changes its features or data handling behavior.

If the policy changes, the updated version will be published in the official GitHub repository or other official distribution channels.
